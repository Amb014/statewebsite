The Makings of Huntsville

Are you looking for a modern city that is booming with new opportunities? If so, Huntsville is the city for you.

In 1811, Huntsville became the first city in Alabama.

Huntsville is located in the northern region of Alabama.

Huntsville has a growing population of over 225,000 residents. It is the largest city in the state of Alabama.

Huntsville is an urban city that is becoming a mecca for the tech industry.

The median family income in Huntsville is $68,0000 which is higher than Alabama's avergage income amount of $31,000.
