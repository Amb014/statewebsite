The Makings of Montgomery

Montgomery is so much more than the capital of Alabama. It's the heart of the Civil Rights Movement.

Montgomery became a city in 1819, and the state capital in 1845.

Montgomery is located in east central Alabama. The roaring waters of the Alabama River run near the the city.

Montogomery has a growing population of over 201,000 residents. The population contiues to grow as the city advances with new developments.

Montgomery is an urban city with a diverse community, unlike the surrounding cities.

The median family income in Montgomery is $54,0000 which is higher than Alabama's avergage income amount of $31,000.
