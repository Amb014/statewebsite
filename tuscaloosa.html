Tuscaloosa

The Makings of Tuscaloosa

Tuscaloosa is the epitome of Southern Charm. In this college city, you will find good sports, good food, and tons of fun activites.

Tuscaloosa became a city in 1819

Tuscaloosa is located in West Alabama. The University of Alabama has been located in Tuscaloosa since 1831.

Tuscaloosa has over 111,000 residents. The college events keeps this city buzzing with visitors.

Tuscaloosa is an urban city with a diverse community, thanks to the university.

The median family income in Tuscaloosa is $47,0000 which is higher than Alabama's avergage income amount of $31,000.
